Get Involved

Here at The Wish Fountain,we have many opportunites for people to join in and get involved in charity work. This is a great chance to meet with like-minded individuals and work towards a good cause. We encourage people from all backgrounds to help in our mission to bring children's dreams to reality.

Volunteer

Join one of hundreds of volunteers around the country in our effort to bring happiness to the world while also making unforgettable memories and friendships! As a volunteer you will be expected to attend training events, assist in fundraising events, and participate in wish visits. To learn more about how to sign up, click on the link below and get started.

Fundraising Events

Every year we have a multitude of fundraiser events which you can participate in! This year our event team has set up marathons, skydiving, hiking fundraisers, bake sales, and a charity football match which anyone can join. To join in, there will be a registration fee. During the events our volunteers will have stands where you can purchase merchandise or make donations.

Donate

Millions of children around the world need your help to make their dreams come true. By donating you will be able to make these terminally ill children's dreams come true! Donations also allow us to keep the organisation up and running and run day to day activities. Make a change by making a monthly or once off donation.
