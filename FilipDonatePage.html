Thank You

A wish granted is true magic for the child, providing respite from their normal routines of hospitals, doctors and treatment. Wherever possible, we invite the whole immediate family to share in the wish, to experience moments of joy and make memories to last a lifetime. We have granted over 9,000 wishes in Ireland, and every wish is unique.

I Wish To Be A Barbie Designer

6 years ago we granted Aideen's wish to be a Barbie fashion designer! Then aged 7, Aideen from Co. Galway was living with myelodysplastic syndrome, and loved all things Barbie. Her special day at Mattel was truly one to remember. At just 4 years old Aideen had to undergo a bone marrow transplant. Mum Rosie described how the current self-isolation situation facing Irish families reflects the isolation and loneliness which they felt while waiting for a transplant. Now 13 years old, we are happy to report that Aideen is in first year of secondary school and doing well!
